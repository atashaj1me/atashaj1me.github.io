Featured

Land-use Right and the Macroeconomy: A DSGE Approach for Vietnam and China Working paper · 2026 · (in progress)

This paper focus on how land-use right, not land, as land is nominally state-owned in Viet Nam and China, play the key role in the macroeconomy as a source of goverment revenues and asset class in the real estate markets. This is a DSGE model inspired by Cochrane (2023)'s "Fiscal Theory of the Price Level," where I treat "land," sovereign bonds, and money supply (via seigniorage) as the goverment's liabilities.
