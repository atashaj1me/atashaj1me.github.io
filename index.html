Fulbright University Vietnam · Ochna Research

I am an undergraduate at Fulbright University Vietnam and a research analyst at Ochna Research. My work sits at the intersection of macroeconomics and private capital: I am interested in how monetary policy, capital flows, and financial frictions shape investment in emerging markets — with a particular focus on Vietnam and Southeast Asia.

On this site you'll find my research, ongoing work in progress, selected investment memos, and a small blog on macro and markets.
